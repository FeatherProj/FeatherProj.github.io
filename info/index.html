Welcome

Welcome to Last Chain, the community-operated Co-op/PvE game server project aims for best Co-op and PvE gameplay experience.

Unlike other community server, we only provide Co-op or PvE game modes.

If you found any rule breakers listed below, please report them to any staff members, we will take care of them. Alternatively, you can use votekick feature to kick player.

Hosting servers costs money. Buying a coffee for us are optional, but please consider doing so to keep our project running in the future!

We hope you enjoy playing on our server! Play nice, play fair and have fun!

Community Rules

To create friendly and enjoyable place for all players, we created the community rules when using our services. Breaking any of the rules may result in a restriction from using our services.

  1. Be nice to others! Any rudeness, harassment, bullying and toxicity to other players are unacceptable.
  2. Respect your/other's privacy!
  3. Do not spam or advertise!
  4. Any bigoted or pornography contents are not permitted! This includes comms(chat/voice chat), sprays, and item decals.
  5. Usage any of third party mods or clients which disrupts the gameplay experience of others are strictly not permitted!
  6. Do not abuse any bugs, glitches, or exploits presented in-game! If you found any of exploits please report them to our staff members.
  7. Do not abuse support command (e.g. !call).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q) History regarding of this project?

When I was middle school student, I began working on the project since 2016 using Minecraft Flan's Mod. However, the project was scrapped in 2017 due to popularity and Oracle's Java SDK policy changes.

After long break until 2018, the project was rebranded as Source Engine server project, but expanded to support more games in the future. After Roon visited many community servers, they were mostly have strict rules, unfriendly community and more bloats even without focusing on gameplay. And also, I looked into several community server's criticism on several places(Such as threads, wiki, etc.), this helped me getting more experiences when managing community server.

After seeing this painful experience through several gameplay server, we decided to make our own several community servers and expand to support more games in the future.

However around autumn of 2021, we decided to cancel the project due to financial issue and taking our time to make decision due to increasement of cheaters and bots on several PvP gamemodes.

On December 11 2021, we finally decided to shift focus on Co-op and PvE gamemodes but with same concept with our past project, since these gamemode has less chance to meet the cheaters in-game and helps many people to enjoy the game together without competing with other players.

Q) When will be released?

We have no set release date, but we are waiting for financial issue to be solved so we can start working on this project.
