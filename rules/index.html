Community Rules

Things to follow when you use our services
Community Rules

This applies to all Mystic Moonlight services.

  1. Be nice to other members! Any toxic behaviors such as harassment, rudeness, racism are not tolerated.
  2. Do not spam on chat or voice chat.
  3. Advertising other website or community is forbidden, which is not related to Mystic Moonlight projects.
  4. Respect your/other's privacy. Never give your/other's personal information to anyone.
  5. NSFW/Offensive contents are forbidden. This includes anything you upload to community.
  6. Do not abuse our support system such as report, CallAdmin, ModMail and so on.
  7. Please refrain from making non-existent rules.
  8. Any scam attempts or unapproved transactions are not allowed. This includes trying to scam other's account, selling account that does not belong to you, etc.
  9. Using alternative account to bypass bans are strictly not allowed. All associated account will be permanently banned.
Playing music through voice chat is allowed except disturbing music.
In-Game Rules
  1. You are not allowed to abuse vote system. This includes votekicking the person without any valid reason.
  2. Some gamemode might have own specific rules. Gamemode Rules can be read here.
  3. You may not abuse in-game bug, glitch or exploits. Instead report them to any staff member.
  4. Do not use unauthorized third-party clients or mods which gains unfair advantage than other players.
Gamemode Rules has more priority than other rules.
Discord Rules
  1. Follow the discord's Terms of Services and Community Guidelines.
  2. You need to be at least 13 years old to join our discord server, according to Discord ToS.
  3. Do not @mention other person without reason.
  4. Post your message on right channel! Read the pinned post, channel info for more information about the current channel.
Any message which breaks Discord Terms of Services or Community Guidelines will be removed immediately without any notice.
Restrictions
  1. You will receive warning if you break the rules for first time, if you already have previous warning for same reason in the past, you will receive restriction such as ban, mute, gag. (e.g. Trolling = Ban, Chat or Voice offense = Gag or mute)
  2. Permanent mute, ban, gag can be given if offense is severe at moderator's discretion. (Such as cheating in-game, ban evasion, scam attempts, etc.)
  3. You may appeal your false restriction on our Discord, using the ModMail. Make sure you include more details about your false warnings and provide evidence if needed.
There will be no second chance for permanent restriction. Only false restrictions can be appealed.
Note
  1. The following rules can be changed at any time with notice.
  2. There is 7 days grace period for rules changes.
  3. You may receive restriction for obstructing the use of the community or doing a clear and malicious act.
There will be no second chance for permanent restriction. Only false restrictions can be appealed.
