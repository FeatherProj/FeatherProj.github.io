Gamemode Rules

Things to follow when you play on specific gamemodes (Last updated on August 23, 2021)
Obsidian Conflict
  1. Map Rushing is not permitted. (If there are 2 or more players in-game, bots are not counted)
  2. Intentional friendly fire[FFNote] on FF Enabled server(Such as Left 4 Dead 2) are not allowed. (If there are 2 or more players in-game, bots are not counted)
  3. Intentional body-blocking is not permitted.
  4. Intentional trolling(Such as destroying the Ally structures which results in team loss, killing the npc which requires to pass from specific area, etc) is not permitted.
Left 4 Dead 2
Co-op
  1. Map Rushing is not permitted. (If there are 2 or more players in-game, bots are not counted)
  2. Intentional friendly fire[FFNote] are not allowed. (If there are 2 or more players in-game, bots are not counted)
  3. Excessive gameplay trolling is not allowed.
Versus
  1. Map Rushing is not permitted. (If there are 2 or more players in-game, bots are not counted)
  2. Do not stall the round. (e.g. As infected, stalling the round by camping inside saferoom)
  3. Intentional friendly fire[FFNote] are not allowed. (If there are 2 or more players in-game, bots are not counted)
  4. Excessive gameplay trolling is not allowed.
Zombie Escape
  1. Stalling the round(Such as camping on specific area) is not permitted.
  2. Since trucing on Zombie Escape can be considered as stalling, trucing with enemy team is not allowed.
  3. Map rushing is not permitted.
  4. Intentional trolling(Such as blocking the way with props) is not allowed.
Machine Attacks
  1. Map Rushing is not permitted. (If there are 2 or more players in-game)
  2. Do not intentionally kill teammates with elevator.
  3. Do not intentionally block enemy robot entrance(e.g. Pyro airblasting, blocking with body, etc) to stall the game.
Mann vs Machines
  1. Do not intentionally block enemy robot entrance(e.g. Pyro airblasting, blocking with body, etc) to stall the game.
VS. Saxton Hale/Freak Fortress
  1. Do not suicide as Boss/Death/Hale.
  2. Do not stall the round.[2]
  3. Do not block the Boss/Death/Hale with Engineer teleporters.
  4. Do not farm rage as Boss/Death/Hale such as farming rage such as taking damage.
Deathrun
  1. Do not suicide as Boss/Death/Hale. Instead type !freerun or clear your queue point before round start.
  2. Do not use engineer structure to gain unfair advantage.
  3. Faking freerun is not allowed. Instead type !freerun or clear your queue point before round start.
  4. Do not stall the round.
Troubles in Terrorist Town
  1. As innocent/detective, do not kill any players on TTT Gamemode without valid reason. (RDM)
  2. You may not use props to kill players.
  3. Ghosting (Giving out tactical information or trying to disrupt the game when spectating) is not allowed.

[FFNote] Sometimes people makes mistake, non-intentional friendly fire will not get restriction.

[2] Engineers are allowed to camp, but only with an active sentry placed down.
