Welcome to The Feather Project.

The Feather Project is the collection of game server projects which aims to focus on best lightweight gameplay experience.

Free for everyone

Tired of donating for features on Community Server? We provide every server features for free, while making donations optional.

Lightweight gameplay experience

We provide best gameplay experience compared to other community servers, without distraction or bloats such as in-game cosmetics and advertisements.

Friendly Community

Our community follows The Libre Project, which includes philosophy when managing community. We have friendly staff members to talk, and listens to your feedback and takes action!
